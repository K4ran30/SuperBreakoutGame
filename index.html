Instructions

-Welcome to Super Breakout Game-

Super Breakout is a action game. The goal is to keep the ball in air using the paddle while breaking as many bricks as you can.

You can change the speed of the ball along with choosing to control the paddle with the arrow keys or with the mouse.

If you are lucky, the ball could have a chance of taking two or more bricks at once.

When the ball hits the ground, the game will be over and you can restart to play the game again. You cannot restart in the middle of a live game!

This is the entire game. Have fun playing!
